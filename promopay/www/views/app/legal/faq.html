2.3 PromoPay Mobile app FAQs

Q: How much does PromoPay Mobile app cost?

A: PromoPay Mobile app is a free app!

Q: What happens if I redeem my voucher by accident?

A: Unfortunately you will not able to get the voucher back if you redeem it by accident. If you press 'redeem voucher' it will be redeemed and will show in your history. Please be very careful not to press redeem until you are ready to use it.

Q: is PromoPay Mobile app available on other phones?

A: We want to make PromoPay Mobile app available on every handset so everyone can enjoy the great savings. Currently PromoPay Mobile app is available on Android and iPhones.

Q: How can I use an offer when there is no Internet or phone signal?

A: You can save you favourite offer in your wallet, by tapping Accept icon in advance when you have a connection. This saves the offer on your device to your wallet so you can use it later when there is no signal or Internet connection available.

Q: How do I use Voucher Codes? A: Once you accept a Voucher it will be available in your wallet, when you want to use this Voucher simply access the Voucher via your wallet and press Redeem, this will display a human readable code and two barcodes. Present this when you are at the cashier.

Q: What are push notifications?

A: Push notifications allow you to be notified as soon as a new offer goes live from your favourite brand.
